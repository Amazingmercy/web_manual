Registrar

Dr. Abubakar Mamuda was born in Zaria on February 23, 1966. He attended Marmara L. E. A. Primary School, Sabon Gari, Zaria, and Government Secondary School, Zaria, now Alhudahuda College in Kaduna State. Mamuda started his career as a university administrator 20 years ago at the University of Abuja, from where he transferred his service to FULafia.
